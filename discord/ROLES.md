# Discord Roles

> **Last updated:** 2026-03-12
> **Server:** Breachers of Tomorrow

## Role Hierarchy

Roles are listed from highest to lowest privilege.

### Leadership Roles

| Role | Purpose | Permissions |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Keepers of The Manifest** | Community leadership team (~15 people) | Full moderation, manage channels, manage roles, access to private Keepers channels |

### Community Roles

| Role | Purpose | How to Get |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Editor** | Has edit access to the community Google Doc | Request in `#list-of-editors` |

> **Note:** Role structure may expand as the community grows. Additional roles (e.g., Wiki Editor, Contributor, Event Organizer) may be added in future phases.

## Role Colors

Current role colors should align with the community's cryoarchive theme palette:

| Color | Hex | Suggested Use |
|-------|-----|---------------|
| Cyan | `#00d4ff` | Keepers / leadership |
| Green | `#00ff9d` | Active contributors |
| Teal | `#7ecfdf` | General members |

## Future Considerations

- **Wiki Editor** role — When Wiki.js launches, editors authenticated via Discord OAuth will get wiki editing privileges
- **Contributor** role — For people who have contributed code, documentation, or significant research
- **Event Organizer** role — For people who run community events
- **Bot** role — For the Discord bot when it's built (Issue [#13](https://github.com/breachers-of-tomorrow/breacher-net/issues/13))
