# Welcome to Breachers of Tomorrow

So you just joined the Discord, found the GitHub, or stumbled across [breacher.net](https://breacher.net) — welcome aboard. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

## What Is This?

**Breachers of Tomorrow** is a community of 1,000+ people built around the [Marathon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marathon_(upcoming_video_game)) ARG (Alternate Reality Game) by Bungie. We collaboratively solve puzzles, track changes to the [cryoarchive.systems](https://cryoarchive.systems) website, and share discoveries.

## What's the ARG?

Bungie launched an in-universe website at [cryoarchive.systems](https://cryoarchive.systems) as part of the buildup to Marathon. The site features:

- **Sectors** that unlock over time
- **Camera feeds** with stabilization levels
- **A kill counter** tracking in-universe events
- **Terminal maps** with explorable areas

Our community tracks all of this in real-time using [breacher.net](https://breacher.net), our custom-built dashboard.

## How to Get Started

### 1. Join Discord

Our home base is Discord: [discord.gg/sGeg5Gx2yM](https://discord.gg/sGeg5Gx2yM)

Key channels:
- **#general** — Main community chat
- **#announcements** — Official updates
- **#rules** — Community guidelines
- **#roles** — Pick up your roles
- **#lfg-breach-protocol** — Find people to play with

### 2. Check the Dashboard

[breacher.net](https://breacher.net) is our live tracker. It shows:
- Current kill count and sector states
- Camera stabilization levels across all sectors
- Detected changes to the cryoarchive website
- Interactive terminal maps

### 3. Read the Research

Our community maintains collaborative research:
- **[Community Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mtUtDPvbh6ahiynYFVS7Z4O79Nw6y5PEOjweCpzWV_A/edit?tab=t.0)** — Active investigation notes
- **[Winnower Garden](https://marathon.winnower.garden/cryoarchive)** — Historical ARG data from day one
- **[Tau Ceti World](https://tauceti.world)** — Interactive colony map

### 4. Contribute

See [CONTRIBUTION-PATHS.md](CONTRIBUTION-PATHS.md) for all the ways you can get involved — from ARG research to code contributions to community organizing.

## Community Structure

The community is organized by **Keepers** — a team of ~15 volunteer leaders who manage Discord, the website, events, and community direction. You'll see them in the `#keepers-of-the-manifest` role on Discord.

## Code of Conduct

Be respectful, be collaborative, and remember we're all here because we love Marathon. Full rules are in the Discord `#rules` channel.
